TANIT is an international congress hosted by AIESEC Carthage, designed to empower delegates through connection, growth, and impactful learning. Get ready for a journey full of unforgettable memories and cultural vibes. The main objective is to help you become a real trainer, with skills that will benefit your AIESEC, personal, and professional life.

Discover the main activities of TANIT 2k25

Become a Trainer

Unlock your potential and learn how to become a certified trainer with international standards.

Football Tournament

Experience the excitement of our football tournament, uniting all LCs and international attendees.

Networking

Connect with passionate youth from around the world and expand your professional and personal network.

Workshops & Sessions

Participate in interactive workshops and sessions led by international facilitators and experts.
